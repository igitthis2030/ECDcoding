Welcome... we are an early learning tech social entreprise based in Johannesburg, South Africa; our core objective is to connect the local ECD sector with federated access to world class coding tools and resources for learning in order to empower teachers and children with the basics of computer science.

Interested ECD managers are welcome to incorporate their current curriculumn with ECDcoding.
In order to diversify and refresh our programme on a regular bases, we work directly with third party 'tech course providers' to collaborate with in rolling out a world class tech education service for all our participants.
